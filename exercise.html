8 Exercises for Weight Loss

Many people try to loss weight each year. Aside from dieting, exercising is one of the most common strategies to shed extra pounds. It burns calories, and is a a key role in weight loss. In addition to helping you lose weight, exercise has been linked to many other benefits, including improved mood, stronger bones, and a reduced risk of many chronic diseases

1. Walking

Walking is a convenient and easy way for beginners to start exercising without feeling overwhelmed or needing to purchase equipment. Also, it’s a lower-impact exercise, meaning it doesn’t stress your joints. It can help reduced body fat and waist circumference. It’s easy to fit walking into your daily routine. To get started, aim to walk for 30 minutes 3–4 times a week. You can gradually increase the duration or frequency of your walks as you become more fit.

2. Jogging or Running

Both jogging and running are great exercises that can be done anywhere and are easy to incorporate into your weekly routine. Studies have shown that jogging and running can help burn belly fat, which is linked to many chronic diseases. To get started, aim to jog for 20–30 minutes 3–4 times per week.

3. Cycling

Cycling is a popular exercise that improves your fitness and can help you lose weight. You can do it indoor or outdoor and it's a low-impact exercise, so it won’t place much stress on your joints.

4. Weight training

Weight training can help you build strength and promote muscle growth. Weight training can also help you lose weight by burning calories during and after your workout.

5. Interval training

Interval training is an effective weight loss strategy that can be applied to many types of exercises, including running, jumping, biking, and more. Incorporating interval training into your routine can help you burn more calories in less time. For example, pedal as hard as you can on a bike for 30 seconds followed by pedaling at a slow pace for 1–2 minutes. Repeat this pattern for 10–30 minutes.

6. Swimming

Swimming is a fun way to lose weight and get in shape. Also swimming is its low-impact nature, meaning that it’s easier on your joints. Moreover, it may help improve your flexibility, reduced body fat, and reduce risk factors for various diseases.

7. Yoga

Yoga is a popular way to exercise and relieve stress. It not only burns calories but also teaches you mindfulness to help you resist food cravings. Additionally, the yoga group experienced improvements in mental and physical well-being.

8. Pilates

Pilates is a great beginner-friendly exercise that may help you lose weight. Other than weight loss, Pilates has been shown to reduce lower back pain and improve your strength, balance, flexibility, endurance, and overall fitness level. Try to combine it with a healthy diet or other forms of exercise, such as weight training or cardio to promoto better weight loss effects.
